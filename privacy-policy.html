Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Tomas Hoca ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Tomas Hoca mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed)
  • Name (optional)

Learning Data

As you use the App, we collect:

  • Lesson progress and completion status
  • Experience points (XP) and streak data
  • Placement test results and learning level
  • Speaking exercise performance metrics (duration, scores). We do not store your actual voice recordings or speech transcripts on our servers.

Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, we collect purchase tokens and transaction identifiers to verify and manage your subscription. Payment processing is handled securely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store).

Analytics Data (Optional)

With your explicit consent, we may collect:

  • App usage data and feature interactions
  • Error reports to improve app stability

You can opt in or out of analytics at any time in the App's Settings.

Device Information

We collect basic device information such as platform type (iOS/Android) and a locally generated device identifier for session management.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our English learning services
  • To track your learning progress and personalize your experience
  • To manage your account and subscriptions
  • To send local notifications about scheduled lessons (with your permission)
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance (with your consent)

3. Microphone and Speech Recognition

Our App uses your device's microphone for speech recognition during speaking exercises. Microphone access requires your explicit permission. Audio is processed in real-time and is not recorded or stored on our servers. Only aggregated performance metrics (such as duration and score) are saved.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security, ensuring you can only access your own data. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS. Chat history and conversation messages are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase - Authentication and data storage
  • Amplitude - Analytics (opt-in only)
  • Sentry - Error tracking (opt-in only)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store - In-app purchases

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We require age verification before allowing access to the App, in compliance with COPPA regulations. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data

When you sign out, all locally stored data is cleared from your device.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our servers within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
